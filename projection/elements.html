Our Brand

This elegant visual identity was designed exclusively for a high-end New York City wedding photography business. The layout is clean and sophisticated, set against a soft, neutral background that emphasizes clarity and elegance. At the top, a minimalist NYC skyline—rendered in rich navy and gold—gracefully encircles a central camera icon, symbolizing the fusion of timeless architecture and contemporary photography. The brand name, “New City”, appears in a luxurious gold script, framing the cityscape with charm and distinction. Below it, “NEW YORK” is displayed in a bold serif font to convey authority and refinement, followed by the subtitle “WEDDING PHOTOGRAPHY” in a clean, modern sans-serif. A final gold line underscores the tagline, “A Sophisticated Photography Business,” perfectly capturing the upscale, artistic vision of the brand.
